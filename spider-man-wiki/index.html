Spider-Man Info

It starts with Peter acquiring spider-related abilities after a bite from a radioactive spider: clinging to surfaces, shooting spider-webs from wrist-mounted devices, and detecting danger with his "spider-sense".

Although a Marvel's super hero, he is famous for his ordinary life: difficulties with friends, family, sweethearts and employers. That inherent humanity, made him "the friendly neighbourhood Spider-Man".

Major villains that appeared in Spider-Man comics in 1964 included Electro, Mysterio, the Green Goblin, and Kraven the Hunter. In 1965 Scorpion appeared. In 1966 Rhino made his debut.

Peter Parker's parents were spies and got killed in a plane crash. Following their deaths, Peter went to live with his Aunt May and Uncle Ben. Later, Uncle Ben is murdered and the comic's most famous caption is attributed in later canon to him, moments before the murder: "With great power there must also come - great responsibility!"
